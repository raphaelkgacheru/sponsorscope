Questions

Common questions.

Which job sites does SponsorScope work on?
Indeed, LinkedIn, Reed, Totaljobs, CV-Library, Glassdoor, NHS Jobs, CWJobs, Adzuna, Guardian Jobs, Engineering Jobs, Technojobs, TechJobs, Caterer, HealthJobsUK, CharityJob, Jobs.ac.uk, New Civil Engineer, and ICE Recruit — with more added regularly.
How current is the sponsor data?
The UK Home Office publishes an updated register weekly. SponsorScope syncs automatically, so you're always working with data that's at most 7 days old.
What's the difference between A-rated and B-rated sponsors?
A-rated sponsors are fully compliant and can hire new sponsored workers. B-rated sponsors are under a Home Office action plan and cannot take on new sponsored workers until they return to A-rated status. SponsorScope flags B-rated companies with a clear warning.
Do I need to create an account?
No account is needed for the core features. You'll need a free Companies House API key — takes 2 minutes to get from the UK government — for the active trading verification.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes — monthly and annual plans can be cancelled any time. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No hidden charges, no awkward cancellation flows.
