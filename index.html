Questions

Common questions.

Which job sites does SponsorScope work on?
Indeed, LinkedIn, Reed, Totaljobs, CV-Library, Glassdoor, NHS Jobs, CWJobs, Adzuna, Guardian Jobs, Engineering Jobs, Technojobs, TechJobs, Caterer, HealthJobsUK, CharityJob, Jobs.ac.uk, New Civil Engineer and ICE Recruit. More sites are added regularly.
How do I get started after paying?
After payment you will land on a setup page with step by step instructions. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store, click the icon, create an account using your payment email, confirm your email address, and sign in. The extension will recognise your subscription and open straight away.
Why do I need to create an account?
Your account is how SponsorScope verifies your subscription. When you create an account using the same email you paid with, the system links your payment to your access automatically. It only takes a minute and you will not need to do it again.
I paid but the extension is asking me to subscribe. What do I do?
This usually happens when the account email does not match the payment email. Sign out of the extension, create a new account using the exact email address you paid with, confirm that email, and sign back in. If you are still stuck, email support@sponsorscope.co.uk and we will sort it out within 24 hours.
How current is the sponsor data?
The UK Home Office publishes an updated register every week. SponsorScope syncs automatically so you are always working with data no more than 7 days old.
What is the difference between A-rated and B-rated sponsors?
A-rated sponsors are fully compliant and can hire new sponsored workers. B-rated sponsors are under a Home Office action plan and cannot take on new sponsored workers until they return to A-rated status. SponsorScope flags B-rated companies with a clear warning on every result.
Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes. Monthly and annual plans can be cancelled at any time from the Manage subscription page inside the extension settings. You keep full access until the end of your current billing period. No hidden charges and no awkward cancellation process.

Every application to a non-sponsor is wasted time. SponsorScope gives you that time back so you only apply where you actually have a chance.
