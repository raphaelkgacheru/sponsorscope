What SponsorScope does

SponsorScope is a Chrome extension that checks whether companies appear on the UK Home Office Register of Licensed Sponsors. It is a research tool designed to help job seekers identify employers who may be able to sponsor a UK work visa.

Accuracy of data

SponsorScope pulls data directly from the UK Home Office and Companies House. However please be aware of the following:

  • The sponsor register updates weekly so there may be a short delay before changes appear in SponsorScope
  • Being on the register does not guarantee a company will offer sponsorship for any specific role
  • SponsorScope is a research tool and does not constitute legal or immigration advice
  • Always confirm sponsor status directly with an employer before making decisions based on this tool
